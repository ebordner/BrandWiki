# Founder profile

## Name and title

## The short bio (use for speaker intros, LinkedIn, pitches)

Elissa Bordner helps venture-backed founders and the investors backing them avoid becoming part of the 95%. She diagnoses the structural collapse points inside product, revenue, and technical infrastructure — and engineers them out before scale magnifies them. In an AI-compressed market, she designs companies that hold at altitude.

## What makes Elissa different from other advisors

She is an **operator**, not a consultant or theorist. The distinction matters deeply to the ICP:
- Founders and GPs categorize operators as people who have done the work, not just advised on it
- Her 25+ years includes hands-on delivery under Fortune 500 pressure where failure meant board consequences
- She does not just recommend — she builds infrastructure and executes

The rare combination: operator credentials + proprietary IP + done-with-you consulting + done-for-you agency execution. Leadership plus execution. That is rare.
