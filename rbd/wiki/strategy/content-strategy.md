# Content strategy

## Primary platforms

| Platform | Role | Content type | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Primary authority platform | Articles + posts | Authority + diagnose curiosity |
| Instagram | Secondary distribution | Posts, reels, carousels | Reach + retargeting pool |
| Podcast appearances | Authority amplification | Guest appearances | Warm new audiences |
| Speaking engagements | Institutional credibility | Conference / accelerator talks | GP + founder access |
| Direct DMs | Conversion | Outreach + follow-up | Qualified conversation |

LinkedIn is primary. The audience (Series A/B founders, VC GPs) lives there. IG is derivative — repurposed LinkedIn content adapted for format.

---

## Content goal

Content is the diagnostic. It does not pitch — it creates tension that makes the offer feel inevitable.

**The content job:** Move people from Asleep → Aware → Slightly Uncomfortable.

Not: "Here's what I offer."
Instead: "Here's what's wrong with how 95% of startups are built — and here's the question you should be asking yourself right now."

---

## Five content pillars

| Pillar | What it covers | Why it resonates |
|---|---|---|
| The 95% problem | Why startups fail, the 10 structural patterns, CB Insights data | Creates the frame and the urgency |
| AI compression | Why AI accelerates structural cracks, not just speed | Timely, urgent, specific to current market conditions |
| Revenue architecture | What the top 5% do differently, MVP+R™, Design Sprints, the 3 Pillars | Positions RbD as the solution |
| Identity / authority | Elissa's POV, refusal hooks, identity statements for ICP | Builds self-selection and loyalty |
| Diagnostic content | Questions founders/GPs should be asking, self-assessment frameworks | Utility without giving the full answer |

## Traffic layers and their roles

**Layer 1 — Organic authority traffic** (warm → semi-warm)
- LinkedIn articles, LinkedIn posts, IG posts and reels, podcast appearances, speaking engagements, direct DMs
- Primary goal: Authority + diagnose curiosity

**Layer 2 — Paid retargeting traffic** (warm)
- IG/Meta retargeting engagers, retargeting masterclass buyers, retargeting site visitors
- Primary goal: Convert to $27 or $222

**Layer 3 — Cold paid traffic** (cold)
- Cold Meta ads, cold LinkedIn ads (future), cold YouTube ads (future)
- Primary goal: Entry offer or VSL for $5K Review

## Ad strategy

**$27 offer:** 12 ad variations, retargeting to content engagers and social followers
**$222 offer:** 12 ad variations, retargeting to $27 buyers and video viewers

**LinkedIn targeting (founders):**
- Titles: Founder, Co-Founder, CEO, Startup Founder
- Company size: 11–500 employees
- Industries: SaaS, AI, Fintech, Computer Software

**LinkedIn targeting (GPs):**
- Titles: General Partner, Managing Partner, Partner
- Company: Venture capital firms

**Tone for all ads:** Institutional. Calm. Specific. Non-desperate. Scarce. High-signal.

## Content voice reminders

- Diagnostic, not motivational
- Short, sharp, structural — use the staccato rhythm for emphasis
- Never over-educate in content (create tension, don't give the full answer)
- Pattern recognition language: "I've seen this pattern..." "Here's what this signals..."
- Refusal hooks as identity statements: "For the founder who refuses to..." "If you're the kind of GP who won't..."
