# Offer architecture

## The full offer stack

| Tier | Offer | Price | Audience | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Organic content / Diagnostic Brief | Free | Cold → warm | Authority + tension creation |
| 1 | MVP+R™ Deep Dive | $27 | Founders (warm) | Entry — self-liquidating commitment |
| 2 | AI Compression & Revenue Architecture Masterclass | $222 | Founders + GPs | Authority deepening + qualification |
| 3 | Revenue Architecture Audit | $5,000 | Serious founders + GPs | First high-ticket gateway — diagnostic |
| 4 | Revenue Architecture Intensive | $35,000+ | Post-review founders | Architecture intervention |
| 5 | Top 5% Council | 4–5 fig quarterly/annual | Post-Intensive + VC firms | Ongoing architecture governance |

**The ascension flow:**
Organic → $27 → $222 → 10-min VSL → $5K Review → $35K+ Intensive → Council

---

## Tier 0: Organic Content (Free)

**What it is:** LinkedIn articles + IG derivative content. The content IS the diagnostic. Exposes the 10 structural failure points, why MVP alone fails, why traction ≠ revenue architecture, why AI compresses weak models.

**Goal:** Moves people from Asleep → Aware → Slightly Uncomfortable. No paywall. Just authority.

**CTA:** Seeds the $27 offer — does not pitch it directly.

**Content hooks used:**
- "If MVP was enough, 95% wouldn't fail."
- "AI accelerates everything — including structural mistakes."
- "Growth exposes fragility."
- "Revenue without architecture is valuation risk."
- "The Top 5% build differently."
- "Is your moat real — or just early?"

---

## Tier 1: MVP+R™ Deep Dive — $27

**Who it's for:** Founders who suspect revenue isn't fully validated.

**Problem it solves:** They validated product. They didn't validate revenue.

**Transformation:** They see:
- Where revenue hypothesis is weak
- Where pricing misaligns with value
- Where margin assumptions fail

**Signature framework:** MVP+R™ = Minimum Viable Product + Revenue validation

**CTA ideas:**
- "Find out if you validated revenue — or just product."
- Retargeting ads to social engagers
- Entry from organic content + comments → DM automation

**Funnel flow:**
Organic Post → Retargeting Ad → $27 Sales Page → Purchase → Post-Purchase Upsell (to $222 OR to 10-min VSL)

**Ascension paths:** $27 → $222 / $27 → 10-min VSL → $5K Review

**Purpose:** Self-liquidating, low-friction commitment. Qualifies buyers.

---

## Tier 2: AI Compression & Revenue Architecture Masterclass — $222

**Full name:** AI Compression & Revenue Architecture: How the Top 5% Build Moats in an Era Where AI Kills Advantage Overnight

**Who it's for:** Founders AND VC GPs who are aware AI is changing the game.

**Problem it solves:** AI accelerates collapse and growth. Most companies don't know which side they're on.

**Format:** 90-minute Executive Masterclass (not a training — a Strategic Briefing for the Top 5%)

**Transformation:**
- AI Compression Scorecard
- Identification of structural exposure
- Understanding common failure traps
- Revenue architecture lens

**Bonus assets:**
- AI Compression Scorecard (PDF diagnostic)
- Revenue Architecture Readiness Checklist
- Board-Ready Question Framework

**Signature concepts taught:**
- AI Compression Reality
- Common Failure Point Elimination
- Revenue Designed Before Scale (The 3 Pillars)

**CTA ideas:**
- "AI doesn't kill startups. Fragility does."
- "Is your revenue structurally durable?"

**Thank You page strategy:** Subtle pre-frame of the Revenue Architecture Audit — "There is a next step. It's architectural diagnosis. When clarity becomes urgent, you'll know." Increases ascension conversion ~20–30%.

**Funnel flow:**
Organic / Retargeting → Sales Page + VSL → Purchase → Masterclass → Ascension Email Sequence → 10-min VSL → Apply for Revenue Architecture Audit

**GP note:** GPs may enter directly at the $222 (GP-specific version) or bypass entirely to the $5K Review via VSL.

---

## Tier 3: Revenue Architecture Audit — $5,000

**Who it's for:** Serious founders and VC GPs. Purchase comes from warm or hot traffic.

**Problem it solves:** Hidden structural exposure. "I feel something's off."

**Deliverables:**
- Exposure mapping
- Revenue logic diagnostics
- Pricing/margin stress testing
- Lifecycle architecture gap analysis
- Tech alignment flags
- AI compression risk flags
- Reinforcement roadmap

**Transformation:** From "I feel something's off" → "Here is exactly where we're exposed and how to fix it."

**Funnel entry:**
- A) Direct Organic → 10-min VSL → Apply
- B) $222 Ascension → 10-min VSL → Apply
- C) Cold Paid → 10-min VSL → Apply
- D) Referral / DM → Call

**Full flow:** Traffic → 10-Minute VSL → Application Page → Qualification Form → Strategy Call → $5K Review

**Ascension:** Review → Revenue Architecture Intensive

---

## Tier 4: Revenue Architecture Intensive — $35,000+

**Who it's for:** Founders post-review, scaling companies serious about redesign. Warm/hot traffic only.

**What it is:** Architecture intervention. Not coaching.

**Deliverables:**
- Product spec alignment
- Pricing/margin redesign
- Lifecycle revenue mapping
- Service design blueprint
- Tech stack architecture diagram
- Revenue logic integration
- AI durability integration
- Board-ready deck

**Transformation:** From "Exposed and reactive" → "Architected and durable."

**Entry sources:**
- Post Revenue Architecture Audit
- Direct high-level referral
- Long-form authority asset (future sales documentary)
- GP referral

**Flow options:**
- Structural Review Complete → Private Strategy Call → Architecture Proposal → Engagement Contract
- Long-Form Authority Film → Application → Call → Intensive

---

## Tier 5: Top 5% Council — 4–5 figure quarterly or annual subscription

**Who it's for:** Companies post the $35K Intensive. VC firms wanting portfolio governance. Pricing based on complexity and scope of product portfolio.

**What it solves:** Ongoing structural drift.

**Deliverables:**
- Quarterly architecture audits
- Margin durability monitoring
- AI compression watch
- Strategic recalibration
- Board-level insight

**Experience:** Long-term architectural governance. Structural discipline under scale.

**Entry:** Post-Intensive invitation OR VC firm governance discussion OR portfolio-level engagement.

---

## Three major conversion assets

1. **10-minute VSL** → $5K Revenue Architecture Audit
2. **Masterclass VSL** → $222 Masterclass
3. **Long-Form Documentary** (future) → $35K Intensive

Everything else feeds these three.

---

## The GP parallel path

GP funnel likely bypasses $27:
Organic Thought Leadership (LinkedIn-heavy) → $222 (GP version) OR direct VSL → Revenue Architecture Audit (Portfolio Lens) → Portfolio-Level Intensive → Council Retainer
