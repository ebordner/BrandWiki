## Open strategic question (from Frank's document)

Frank's document raises this directly:

> *"I want your opinion, should we change this $5K offer page to the new $222 Revenue Architecture Diagnostic?"*

**My read on this:**

Keep the $5K Revenue Architecture Audit as the primary conversion page. Here's why:

The $5K audit page is a **high-conviction closer** — it's doing the job of converting the most serious buyers, the ones with real money and real urgency. The copy is strong, the deliverables are specific, the transformation is clear. That job shouldn't be diluted.

The $222 Diagnostic is better served with **its own dedicated page** at a lower barrier — it converts the middle of the funnel (people who engaged with $27 or $47 content and are ready to invest more but aren't ready for $5K). Give it a page, drive warm traffic to it, and let the $222 buyers ascend naturally to the $5K audit.

**In other words: don't replace the $5K page. Add a $222 page.**

The funnel logic should be:
- $5K page = primary CTA for all high-conviction entry points (Salesumentary, direct GP outreach, referrals)
- $222 page = secondary CTA for warm organic traffic and $27/$47 buyers ascending
