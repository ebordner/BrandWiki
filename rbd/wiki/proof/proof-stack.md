# Proof stack

## The headline proof point

**Panoramic WiFi:** Inherited a product that had sold 13 units at $129 to 4 million customers. Rebuilt using The Profitable Method™. Launched nationally on Super Bowl Sunday, 2017 with a Super Bowl commercial. Result: **1 million customers and $100M in year one.** The product still generates monthly revenue today — total lifetime value in the hundreds of millions from one launch.

*Why this proof works:* It's not a startup success story. It's a Fortune 500 product that had already failed — that was rebuilt using the method and became a $100M launch in one year. Proof the method works with real capital under real board and investor pressure.
