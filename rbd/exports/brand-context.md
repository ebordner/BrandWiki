## Our intellectual property

### The Profitable Method™
A 10-step acrostic framework — P-R-O-F-I-T-A-B-L-E — addressing every major startup failure point (CB Insights, 110+ postmortems). Each letter:

- **P** — Perceive the full market landscape
- **R** — Research with empathy (consumer research)
- **O** — Optimize the financial model (flip: financial model drives product requirements)
- **F** — Facilitate market AND technical validation simultaneously
- **I** — Improve the offering with a service strategy (build service blueprint alongside product)
- **T** — Tie together requirements, financial model, and tech architecture
- **A** — Accelerate product and service iterations via design sprint
- **B** — Begin development only after a gate check
- **L** — Launch development of product AND service blueprint
- **E** — Execute the launch with success

Published in *The Profitable Startup* (Elissa Bordner, 2022, Difference Press).

### MVP+R™
Minimum Viable Product + Revenue. Revenue must be validated simultaneously with product, not discovered after traction. "Revenue should not be discovered accidentally after traction. It should be designed intentionally before scale." MVP validates engagement. MVP+R™ validates durability.

### Three delivery pillars
1. **Design Sprints (Reengineered)** — Decision velocity, not brainstorming. Collapses revision cycles from 3 years to 3 days.
2. **Flipping the Financial Model** — Financial model drives product requirements. Every feature maps to margin logic.
3. **Service Design Blueprints** — End-to-end mapping: Learn → Buy → Get → Use → Pay → Support.
